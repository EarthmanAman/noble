Mission

My mission is to help you through my life experience and knowledge, be the best version of yourself from a place of inner well being and to impact a God fearing future focused generation with strong ethos, unwaived Integrity and unshakeable stability both physical and emotional.

My Story

Makandi is a Meru name with different meanings, among them a go-getter and daring person. To a Makandi nothing is impossible; she will go where no one dares to go. With that knowledge would you be surprised why I would go from one thriving career to another and leave at the peak of them all?

Who am I

With over a decade experience in corporate United Kingdom ranging from Retail, Ministry Of Defence (UK), Telecommunications, Management and Hospitality – I have gathered major skills, experiences and with my charisma you may dare call me a jack of all trades. I am a go-getter and not afraid to start all over because in that starting, I know better and will be better.

With time and occupation in different fields, I realised that I turned out to be great at teaching and always wanted to help others release their full potential and a higher self – a better version of themselves. Seeing people in full potential brought satisfaction. That is when the penny dropped! It is my passion and talent brought to one.
I always had it just didn’t know what it was.

Life coaching is about creating the right mindset and to help you fill the gaps in our master plan and bring about clarity and accountability. Life Coaching is action and future focused.
